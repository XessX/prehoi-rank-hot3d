# Journal Route

Primary target:

- Machine Learning with Applications, Elsevier

Why it fits:

- Peer-reviewed, fully open access journal focused on machine learning.
- Scope includes computer vision, NLP, intelligent systems, neural networks, AI-based software engineering, and applied ML.
- Accepts regular papers and technical notes.
- Encourages reproducible research objects and public repositories.

Research4Life route:

- Treat this as a submission-planning path, not a guaranteed outcome.
- Before submission, verify current Elsevier Research4Life waiver/discount policy.
- Confirm that author affiliations, corresponding author status, country eligibility, and funding status satisfy the current policy.
- Keep all claims about APC waiver eligibility out of the methods/results sections.
